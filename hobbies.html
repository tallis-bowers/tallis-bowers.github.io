Board Games

Board games are a wonderfully unique social experience. Their themes offer a small degree of shared fantasy between players, and their thematic art can often be appreciated independently of the game itself. Far from purely aesthetic, however, board games ask their players to develop new ways of thinking and to consider the personalities and minds of their opponents or allies.

Favorite games: Settlers of Catan, Smallword, 7 Wonders Duel, Clank!, and Jaipur
